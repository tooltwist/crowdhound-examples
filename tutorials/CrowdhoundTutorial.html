Crowdhound Tutorial

Introduction

This tutorial will teach you how to add Crowdhound functionality to your website.

Part One takes about five minutes, and will have you adding default functionality for comments and discussions to your site.

Part Two takes about half an hour, and will show you how to add a blog, chat, a Q&A section and FAQs to your website.

Part Three may take half a day, but during that time you will create templates and alter the build pipeline to customize your site to your heart's desire.

Part Four contains various topics and cookbooks, from subject ranging from event calendars and loading images with a phone camera through to building a fully collaborative teching environment.
